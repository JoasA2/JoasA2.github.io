Movie List

  • Annabelle

    A horror movie about a cursed doll that brings terror to its owners.

  • The Grudge

    A terrifying story of a curse born when someone dies in the grip of rage.

  • A Nightmare on Elm Street

    Teens are haunted by Freddy Krueger, a clawed killer who attacks in their dreams.

  • The Hills Have Eyes

    A family is terrorized by a group of violent cannibals in a desert wasteland.

  • Hostel

    Travelers are trapped and tortured in a sinister underground organization.

  • Midsommar

    A group of friends visit a rural Swedish festival that quickly turns disturbing.

  • Tusk

    A podcaster is held captive by a man with a horrific plan to turn him into a walrus.

  • House of 1000 Corpses

    A tale of a group of friends who encounter a family of sadistic killers.

  • The Human Centipede

    A mad scientist surgically connects people into a single digestive system.

  • Saw

    People are placed in deadly traps as part of a twisted game to test their will to survive.

  • Gummo

    A disturbing depiction of life in a small, poor town devastated by a tornado.

  • Irreversible

    A story told in reverse about a horrific crime and its aftermath.
